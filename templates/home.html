About NEFT

National Electronic Funds Transfer (NEFT) is an India-wide electronic payment system facilitating one-to-one funds transfer. Managed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), it allows individuals, firms, and corporations to electronically transfer funds from any bank branch to any individual, firm, or corporation having an account with any other bank branch in the country participating in the Scheme.

NEFT operates in half-hourly batches throughout the day, 24x7, 365 days a year. This system is widely used for various transactions, including salary payments, loan EMIs, credit card payments, and other bill payments, offering a secure and efficient way to move money across India.
